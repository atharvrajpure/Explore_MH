- The wealthiest and most developed state in India, Maharashtra, has a wide variety of tourist attractions, national parks, bird sanctuaries, and leisure pursuits including paragliding, scuba diving, and trekking, Camping, mountaineering, wild cave explorations, valley crossing, bungee jumping, rock climbing, bird-watching, etc are the most exciting adventure activities in Maharashtra.

- Adventure travel is a type of tourism, involving exploration or travel with a certain degree of risk (real or perceived), and which may require special skills and physical exertion.

- Adventure sports tourism has traditionally been dominated by men. Although women's participation has grown, the gender gap is still pronounced in terms of quantitative engagement in these forms of sport tourism. Yet, in competitive adventure sport tourism, the success rate of females is currently higher than that of males



-Adventure in Maharastra-


1. Paragliding

- There are several locations for paragliding in Maharashtra like Talegaon Dabhad, Virar, Brahmanwadi, Saputara and Nashik, which offer ideal setting.

- Kamshet is one among the most popular destinations for paragliding that is often visited by adventure freaks to take pleasure in this outdoor adventure sport.

-Also known as the home for paragliding in Maharahtra, Kamshet offers world-class paragliding facilities round the year.

2. Scuba Diving

- Maharashtra is a paradise for scuba divers that offers a golden chance to enjoy a very enthralling water sport. One of the most exotic adventure sports, scuba diving tops the things to do list in the state, where all one needs for it, is a little bit of briefing, and the right gears.

- In Maharashtra, you can scuba dive at picturesque locations that are also adorned with archaic forts, warbling waterfalls, virgin beaches and lush green mountains.

- Devbaug and Murud in Maharashtra also offer great opportunities for this amazing water sport.

3. River Rafting

- A paradise for river rafters, Maharashtra serves as a treat to countless adventure freaks who journey to this state to quench their thirst for adventure. Dotted with mesmeric locations for adrenaline filled with white water river rafting, this travel destination offers the perfect setting to take pleasure in this engaging water sport.

- One among the most elating adventure sports, it is packed with utmost thrill and tons of excitement.

-Rafting on River Kundalika at Kolad will be an experience in itself. Located away from the hustle and bustle, Kundalika is a haven for river rafting.

4. Rappelling

- Maharashtra, one of the most sought after travel destinations, is ideal for experiencing thrills of some of the most wild and whacky adventure sports. Rappelling is one such adventure sport that tops the things to do list of countless adventure freaks.

- Konkan Kada cliff at Harishchandragad, Dukes Nose Lonavala, Sandhan Valley near Ratangad and Karjat near Mumbai are best destinations to indulge in this madcap adventure sport.

-With the increasing popularity of rappelling, Maharashtra tourism also conducts this nerve-wrecking adventure sport for lovers of adventure

5. Trekking

- Maharashtra, known for its vivacious metropolis, is also a lot famous for trekking expeditions amidst captivating natural beauty and wilderness.

-There are several treks in Maharashtra that lend you with an incredible experience. These treks are the ones which offer new dimensions to existing natural wonders

-The adventure freaks can set off for hiking and can also enjoy rappelling amidst an ethereal backdrop during this trek.
